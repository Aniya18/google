GROW WITH GOOGLE BUSINESS MEETUP

#GrowWithGoogle

Pinch me I’m dreaming! I still can’t believe I, at 16, was able to represent Zume Inc. at Googleplex-Google’s headquarters- for the Small Business Meetup. I had high expectations of what Google would be like considering I watched The Internship on the plane ride to California; yet, google still managed to exceeded all of my expectations.

At this three-day event, I along with 49 other businesses from across the country attended various sessions where we learned how Google could help with growing a business. In these sessions I learned about improving a customer’s mobile experience, understanding Google analytics, and other platforms Google has to support businesses.

While Google taught me a lot, I also learned from other business owners as well. I was intrigued by their stories of an idea that became a mission, and a mission that became a business. After these three days, I became even more inspired and excited about starting my own business. I will surely be signing yearbooks:
